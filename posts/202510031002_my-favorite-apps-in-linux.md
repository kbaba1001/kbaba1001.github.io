* Linux: Gentoo Linux
* Terminal: [Alacritty](https://alacritty.org/config-alacritty.html)
* Editor: VS Code, vim
* ブートローダー: systemd-boot (not grub)
* GUI: X (not wayland)
* Window Manager: i3
* Screen: tmux
* Browser: Brave
* Clipboard History: clipmenud
* IM: fcitx5 + Mozc
* display management: arandr
* Audio Mixer: alsamixer
* TUI Apps
  * enhancd
  * bat
  * ripgrep
  * safe-rm
  * fzf
  * keychain
